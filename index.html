By Sean Smith

Description:

This is a simple recipe for a classic grilled cheese sandwich. It's quick, easy, and delicious! With only two slices of bread, 2 slices of cheese, and some butter, you can make yourself a zesty American snack.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Instructions:

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread
  2. Place one slice of bread, butter side down, in a pan over medium heat
  3. Place cheese slices on top of bread
  4. Place second slice of bread, butter side up, on top of cheese
  5. Cook until bread is golden brown
  6. Flip and cook until other side is golden brown
  7. Remove from pan and let cool for a few minutes
  8. Cut that grilled cheese in half and enjoy!

Enjoy your delicious grilled cheese sandwich!
